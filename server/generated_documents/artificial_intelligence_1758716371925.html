Artificial Intelligence: A Comprehensive Overview

Slide 1: What is Artificial Intelligence?

  • Mimicking human intelligence in machines
  • Focus on learning, reasoning, and self-correction
  • Applications across diverse fields

Slide 2: Types of AI

  • Narrow or Weak AI (specific tasks)
  • General or Strong AI (human-level intelligence)
  • Super AI (surpassing human intelligence)

Slide 3: AI Applications in Action

  • Healthcare (diagnosis, drug discovery)
  • Finance (fraud detection, algorithmic trading)
  • Transportation (self-driving cars)

Slide 4: Ethical Considerations of AI

  • Bias and fairness in algorithms
  • Job displacement and economic impact
  • Privacy and data security concerns
